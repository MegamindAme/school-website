About Us

Why We Are Better

What makes us stand out among all the primary schools in South Africa.

Anjuman Schools have a long history of providing quality education to students from all backgrounds.
The schools were established in the early 1900s and have since grown to become one of the most respected educational institutions in South Africa. They have a reputation for providing quality education to students from all backgrounds, regardless of their race, religion, or socio-economic status.
The schools have a strong focus on academic excellence and provide students with a well-rounded education.
Anjuman Schools offer a comprehensive curriculum that includes academic subjects, as well as extracurricular activities such as sports, music, and drama. The schools have a team of highly qualified teachers who are dedicated to providing students with the best possible education.
Anjuman Schools have a diverse student body, which helps to promote cultural understanding and tolerance.
The schools attract students from a wide range of backgrounds, which helps to create a diverse and inclusive learning environment. This diversity helps to promote cultural understanding and tolerance among students, which is an important skill in today’s globalized world.
The schools have a strong commitment to community service and encourage students to get involved in volunteer work.
Anjuman Schools believe in giving back to the community and encourage students to get involved in volunteer work. This helps to instill a sense of social responsibility in students and teaches them the importance of giving back to their communities.
Anjuman Schools have a strong track record of producing successful graduates who go on to make a positive impact in their communities.
The schools have a reputation for producing graduates who are well-prepared for the challenges of the real world. Many of these graduates go on to make a positive impact in their communities, whether through their careers, volunteer work, or other endeavors.

Word from the director.

Anjuman Islam Primary School is a public school with Islamic ethics that enrolls learners from grade RR to 7.

Our school caters for the needs of learners from diverse backgrounds. We are proud of our multi-cultural environment and enterprising culture.

Our vision is "To become the preferred school that is the center of Excellence"

Our mission is " To create a leader in every learner". We have a dedicated and enthusiastic teaching team which continuously strives to ensure that learners reach their full potential and achieve academic excellence.

We strive to entrench the values, competencies and knowledge required for success in the 21st century.

We are dedicated to our mission and vision of educating the whole child in a disciplined Islamic environment and shaping them into becoming leaders of tomorrow.

Our leadership programs we designed with the expectation on that our learners will make a positive contribution to Anjuman Islam Primary school to society at large.

Great emphasis is placed on the inculcation of our 9 Core values which is designed to encourage the holistic development of our learners with high expectations of good discipline and impeccable moral values.

The development of our school over the past few years has been significant and, in the future, we hope to build on the firm foundation which have been established.

We value our close working relationship with parents, caregivers and wider community and are appreciative of the support we receive.
